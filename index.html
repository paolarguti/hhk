Responsible Farming

We share our customer's food concerns and consider them on everything we do and with the standards and expectations we set with our farmer partners. We are here to provide you the best, most nutritious local food.

Sustainably Grown

We take pride in being good stewards of the land.

All of our land is under agricultural preservation. We use cover crops to protect from erosion and use non-GMO seeds with proper crop rotation.

Born and raised on the farm

Our dairy and beef herd are born and raised on the farm. Our laying chickens are cage-free and certified humanely raised.

Our cows spend their days on pasture. Fresh air, sunlight, and endless grass is provided for them. In addition to grazing, we feed them a nutritious mix of the crops we grow ourselves.

Come help feed the calves!

Our calves are bottle fed every day at 4pm in our calf barn. Bottle feeding them ensures they get the proper nutrition and plenty of food to grow on. Arrive early if you are interested in participating!

Located in the heart of the Civil War Heritage area, South Mountain Creamery is open year round (closed major holidays), rain or shine! Guests are welcome to visit and take a tour of the property, along with taking in the calf feeding and other daily scheduled activities!
